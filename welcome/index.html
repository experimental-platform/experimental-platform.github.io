The 5 Minute Promise

Installing platform and creating your first app only takes a couple of minutes.
Your app will instantly be available on the internet while it's still hosted on your local machine.

  1. Follow these instructions to install CoreOS Linux on your local hardware.

    Read our hardware requirements first. Alternatively you can install it in a local VM or simply order pre-installed hardware from us.

  2. Install Experimental Platform by executing this one-liner on the hardware:

    curl https://raw.githubusercontent.com/experimental-platform/platform-configure-script/master/platform-configure.sh | sudo PLATFORM_INSTALL_OSUPDATE=true PLATFORM_INSTALL_REBOOT=true PLATFORM_SYS_GROUP=protonet sh

  3. Open http://paleale.local/ (or the corresponding IP) in your browser and setup Experimental Platform.

    You will be asked for a name, password and your public ssh key.

  4. Now deploy your first "hello world" app from your computer:

    git clone https://github.com/experimental-platform/nodejs-hello-world.git && cd nodejs-hello-world git remote add platform ssh://dokku@your-box.local:8022/nodejs-hello-world git push platform master

  5. That's it! Your app is now available on the internet and within your local network.

    Locally via http://your-name.local/nodejs-hello-world/ and remotely via https://your-name.protonet.info/nodejs-hello-world/
